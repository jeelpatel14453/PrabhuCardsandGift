About Us

Your Local Gift Shop with Heart

At Prabhu Cards & Gifts, we make sure that every gift has a special and unique meaning for its recipient. We value our customers' satisfaction when it comes to their loved ones. Located on County Road 111, we help our Manorville neighbors find perfectly curated gifts for their family and friends.

Whether it's greeting cards, unique gifts, crucial wrapping supplies, or friendly service you can count on — our team is committed to help you. Whether it's a last-minute birthday card, stocking up on holiday décor, or finding the perfect present for someone special, we are here to help with browsing and wrapping.

Although we are a store you can count on, we are also a part of the community. Stop in and greet us, and let us help you make every occasion personal.
